Books for a 4 year old

Dream Little One, Dream by Sally Morgan and Ambelin Kwaymullina

Insects buzz a story of the earth

Magic Beach by Alison Lester

I live near the beach, and it is indeed magic. Probably my single favourite book to read to my daughter. I can (and do) recite this book from memory, word-for-word. Alison's illustration style is fantastic ('ink and wash', I think), and her books are full of imagination. We have several, and they're all good, but Magic Beach is best of all.
