Chris Botman's homepage

Welcome to my homepage! A small place on the web I can go full-nerd. This is not a blog, we're going old-school here.

I'm an aspiring indie game developer, sharing my game(s) and some of the inspiration behind them. Hopefully this will weave together threads of interest that are well suited to sharing on the web: retro gaming (especially PS1 and PC), fantasy and sci-fi (e.g. D&D, Star Trek TNG, X-Files) and a bit of Japanese pop-culture. In other words, I'm a millennial (or Gen Y, as we used to be called) nerd, bitten by the nostalgia bug.

My homepage is not an attempt to document all of my interests or everything I do. I'm engaged in many things: being a dad and husband, hobbies (gardening, art, music, walking and reading), work, and trying to leave Earth a better place than I found it (not going well). Some links you might like, if you like things I also like.

Electronic Gaming Monthly (EGM) magazine scans
EGM was a popular console game magazine my older brother and I read at high school in the late '90s. Scans of every issue are available! Go back to a time before mobile phones, revisit the emergence of the web, enjoy fan art sent in the mail, but most of all, relive the glory of PSX, N64 and more (Sega consoles *sniff*).
Metal Jesus Rocks
Everybody's favourite long-haired, metal loving retro game collector, and a nice guy to boot. I've decided not to try to collect physical games and stick to emulation, but I am happy to partake in MJR sharing his passion.
