15th April, 2007

Amerikamura, Dotonbori, and Den Den Town

These photos and onwards I've uploaded since getting back to New Zealand, so the captions will be as best as I remember what I was taking a photo of…

Between these photos and when I visited Nara, I mostly just stuffed around Osaka, checking out shops (especially music and book stores (I wish I could read Japanese)), etc.

Kids playing Saturday morning baseball at the local prison primary school (I'm walking to the train station).

Amerikamura. I guess it means America Town. Lots of Bob Marley stuff for sale here.

One end of Dotonbori. This is where all the flashy lights and stuff are at night (pics of that later). This area influenced Blade Runner.

Hmm, that girl facing the camera is cute, I should have paid more attention.

(Roll your mouse on and off the crab.)

The best thing about this picture is it gives me a chance to say the words "eye stalks". The crab has moving eye stalks.

So the reason I was in town this day was to visit Den Den Town. However, it was one of the very few places in Japan that disappointed me.

The area is billed as an electronics district, and yeah, there were some computer and camera shops there, but half the shops were just porn (mostly hentai). Typical shops would be computers or toys on floor one, dodgy toys down the back, and three floors of porn above.

Saw some Maid Cafes but didn't go in any (honest)! They look cute, but I'm not comfortable being waited on, let alone by a girl dressed as a French maid pretending to be my little-sister.

So yeh, it was disappointing to see what could have been a geek haven to be so sleezy. Who buys porn anyway, don't they have the internet?
